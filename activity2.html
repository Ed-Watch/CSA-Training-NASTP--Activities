Activity 2: Scenario Analysis Boardroom Decision Game

✓

Objective

Apply climate resilience and strategic thinking to boardroom decisions. Analyze three climate scenarios and make investment decisions as a power company management team.

⏱

Duration

10–15 minutes. Answer strategic questions based on different climate scenarios to test your decision-making skills.

Your Challenge: Regional Power Company

You are the management team facing three possible futures. Review each scenario.

Scenario A: Orderly Transition

  • ✓ Carbon tax introduced gradually
  • ✓ Renewable energy incentives increase
  • ✓ Investor pressure rises steadily
  • → Implication: Predictable regulatory environment

Scenario B: Disorderly Transition

  • ✗ Carbon tax delayed for years
  • ✗ Suddenly implemented at high rate
  • ✗ Fossil-fuel assets rapidly lose value
  • → Implication: Stranded assets risk, sudden disruption

Scenario C: Hot House World

  • ✗ Weak climate policy
  • ✗ Frequent floods and heatwaves
  • ✗ Grid disruptions increase
  • → Implication: Physical climate risks dominate

Strategic Poll Questions

Select your answers for each scenario-based question

1 Under Scenario A (Orderly Transition), where should the company invest first?

2 Under Scenario B (Disorderly Transition), which asset is most vulnerable?

3 Under Scenario C (Hot House World), which action improves resilience most?
