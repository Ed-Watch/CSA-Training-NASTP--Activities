💡 Key Takeaways
  • • Physical risk = damage caused by climate events (floods, fires, drought)
  • • Transition risk = economic impact of shifting to low-carbon economy (policy, tech, market)
  • • NGFS Portal & IEA are scenario frameworks; ERA5 is observed climate data; MSCI Climate is financial/sector data
